AI/ML Machining Time Prediction - Reimagined

Had problem where CNC machining times were widely varying and could not predict finsh dates.
Reimaginded the prediction to be factory network of sensors storing to a database and training AI/ML model.

Summary of Video:

AI CNC Machining Time
Problem Statement
Had CNC machining times that varied from 20 hours to 250+ hours
Couldn’t price the jobs properly
Couldn’t schedule shop time
More importantly couldn’t give accurate delivery times
Reimagine
Reimagine - Drawing of sensors on the machine
Reimagine - I/O used
ReImagine - System Architecture
Reimagine - AI model
Reimagine - AI flowchart
Create controllers to gather data on the CNC machine
Create network to take data and move to a database
Add environmental sensors: weather, temperature, humidity
Create app that converts drawing to numerical values
_ number and length of straight lines
_ number of and radii of arcs
Closed loop with feedback back to model
Outcome
Was able to determine machining times to within 3 hours
Employees were able to schedule their work schedules better
No more rushing at the last minute
More importantly, was able to accurately give a delivery date to customer
Lessons Learned
Have to begin storing data as soon as possible
Even before AI models are fully built
Converting data to numerical values takes a lot of thought
For customers, one of the top priorities is accurate delivery times
